# Double Slap
*Normal | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 1
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Successive Actions. -2 Accuracy.

> The user attacks its foe with two or more slaps in the face.
