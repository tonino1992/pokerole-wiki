# Trapinch (#0328)

<img src="../../assets/images/pokemon/328.png" align="right" width="220" style="margin-left:20px;margin-bottom:8px;">

*Ant Pit Pokemon*

**Type:** Terra
**Abilities:** [[Hyper Cutter]], [[Arena Trap]], [[Sheer Force]] *(Hidden)*
