# Alakazam (#0065)

<img src="../../assets/images/pokemon/065.png" align="right" width="220" style="margin-left:20px;margin-bottom:8px;">

**Type:** Psychic
**Abilities:** [[Synchronize]], [[Inner Focus]], [[Magic Guard]] *(Hidden)*
