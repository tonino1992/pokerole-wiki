# False Surrender
*Dark | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Insight + Allure
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Never Fail.

> The Pokemon bows in defeat, begging for forgiveness, then backstabs its gullible foe.
