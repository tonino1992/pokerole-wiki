# Belly Drum
*Normal | Support*

- **Accuracy:** Tough + Perform
- **Damage:** N/A
- **Target:** User
- **Effect:** User deals Damage to itself equal to Half of its total HP rounded down. Increase User's Strength by 3.

> The Pokemon Roars a war cry while hitting its Belly.
