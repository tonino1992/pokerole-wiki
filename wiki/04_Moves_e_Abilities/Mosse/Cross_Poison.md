# Cross Poison
*Poison | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** High Critical. Roll 1 Chance Dice to Poison the foe.

> The Pokemon drenches poison into its pincers to slash the enemy in a brutal way.
