# Fiery Dance
*Fire | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Perform
- **Damage:** Special + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 5 Chance Dice to Increase the User's Special.

> The user cloaks itself in flames and shoots them while dancing gracefully.
