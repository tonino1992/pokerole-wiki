# Dragon Ascent
*Flying | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Strength + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 5
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Lethal. Reduce the user's Defense and Sp. Defense.

> The user rises above the clouds and then comes crashing down against its foe in an instant. The strike is brutal for both.
