# Accelerock
*Rock | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Priority 1.

> The Pokemon dashes at great speed using the rocks on its body to strike its foe upon impact.
