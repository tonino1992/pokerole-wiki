# Crafty Shield
*Fairy | Support*

- **Accuracy:** Insight + Nature
- **Damage:** N/A
- **Target:** User and Allies
- **Effect:** Priority 3. The User and its Allies are protected from the effects of a Support Move. Shield.

> The Pokemon creates a magic shield to protect everyone from the bad tricks a foe might have under its sleeve. Under this shield, everyone can see dishonesty and bad intentions.
