# Fire Fang
*Fire | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 2 Chance to Flinch the Foe. Roll 2 Chance Dice to Burn the foe. -1 Accuracy.

> The user breathes fire from its mouth while biting the target.
