# Bug Buzz
*Bug | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Special + Perform
- **Damage:** Special + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Sound Based. Roll 1 Chance Dice to reduce foe's Sp. Defense.

> The Pokemon uses its wings or part of its body to emit sound waves that harm and affect the target.
