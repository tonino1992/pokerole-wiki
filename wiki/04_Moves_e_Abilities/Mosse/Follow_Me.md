# Follow Me
*Normal | Support*

- **Accuracy:** Cool + Perform
- **Damage:** N/A
- **Target:** User
- **Effect:** Foes will target the user this Round. Priority 5.

> The Pokemon will draw everyone's attention. All foes are taunted to do their worst.
