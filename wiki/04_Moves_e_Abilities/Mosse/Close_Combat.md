# Close Combat
*Fighting | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Strength + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 5
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** After dealing damage, Reduce the User's Defense and Sp. Defense.

> The user attacks at a close distance to deal lots of damage. The foe is also close enough to attack.
