# Flame Wheel
*Fire | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 1 Chance Dice to Burn the foe.

> The user cloaks itself in flames and rolls to tackle the target.
