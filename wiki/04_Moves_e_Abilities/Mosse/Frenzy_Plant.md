# Frenzy Plant
*Grass | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Special + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 6
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Must Recharge. -1 Accuracy.

> The user roots down and calls the plants to grow in a frenzy around the foe, though it leaves the user exhausted.
