# Brave Bird
*Flying | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Strength + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 5
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Recoil.

> The Pokemon flies straight at the foe at full speed, the collision might hurt both of them.
