# Body Slam
*Normal | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 3 Chance Dice to Paralyze the foe.

> The user charges against the foe using its body to knock it down, leaving the target stunned.
