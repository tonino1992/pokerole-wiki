# Bonemerang
*Ground | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Ranged. Double Action. -1 Accuracy.

> The Pokemon throws a bone to hit the foe. The bone returns like a boomerang, striking a second time.
