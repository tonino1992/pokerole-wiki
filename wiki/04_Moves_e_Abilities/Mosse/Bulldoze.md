# Bulldoze
*Ground | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 2
- **Target:** Area
- **Effect:** Reduce the Dexterity of those affected.

> The user stomps and tramples the place, breaking stones, trees, and everything nearby.
