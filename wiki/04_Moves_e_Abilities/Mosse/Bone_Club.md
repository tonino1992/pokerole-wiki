# Bone Club
*Ground | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 2 Chance Dice to Flinch the Foe. -2 Accuracy.

> The user hits the target with a bone, the smack may leave a bump on the target's head.
