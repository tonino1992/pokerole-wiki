# Acid Spray
*Poison | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Reduce the foe's Special by 2.

> The user spits a corrosive substance, the burning sensation doesn't allow it to focus on attacking.
