# Blaze Kick
*Fire | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** High Critical. Roll 2 Chance Dice to Apply Burn 2 to the Foe. -1 Accuracy.

> The user throws a mighty kick engulfed in fire that may leave a bad looking burn.
