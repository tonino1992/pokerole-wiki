# Flamethrower
*Fire | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 1 Chance Dice to Apply Burn 2 to the foe.

> The user lets out a powerful fire stream that leaves a scorched trail.
