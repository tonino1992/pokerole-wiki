# Barrage
*Normal | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 1
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Ranged. Successive Actions. -1 Accuracy.

> The Pokemon Throws many round objects at the target, one after the other.
