# Freezy Frost
*Ice | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Special + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** If successful, remove all Increased or Reduced Attributes and Traits the Foe may have. If the user of this Move is at its Final Stage of Evolution, this move fails. -1 Accuracy.

> The user touches its foe while playing "Freeze!". Surprisingly, the foe becomes frozen on a block of ice. Once they realize they were just playing pretend the ice dissolves into haze.
