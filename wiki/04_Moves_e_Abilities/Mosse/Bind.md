# Bind
*Normal | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 1
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Block. Deal 2 dice of damage at the end of each Round. Lasts 4 Rounds. -2 Accuracy.

> The Pokemon uses its body to bind the foe, restraining and squeezing it.
