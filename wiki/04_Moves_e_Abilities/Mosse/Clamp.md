# Clamp
*Water | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Block. Deal 2 Dice of Damage at the end of each Round. Lasts 4 Rounds. -1 Accuracy.

> The user traps and crushes the opponent using its thick and sturdy shell.
