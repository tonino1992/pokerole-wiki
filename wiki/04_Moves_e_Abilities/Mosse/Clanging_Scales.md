# Clanging Scales
*Dragon | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Perform
- **Damage:** Special + 4
- **Target:** All Foes
- **Effect:** Sound Based. Reduce User's Defense.

> The Pokemon rattles the scales of its armor creating a frightening noise in the area. Some of its scales fall of as a result, leaving some areas exposed.
