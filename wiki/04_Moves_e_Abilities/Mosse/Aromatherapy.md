# Aromatherapy
*Grass | Support*

- **Accuracy:** Insight + Nature
- **Damage:** N/A
- **Target:** User and Allies
- **Effect:** Cure User and Allies from Burn, Freeze, Paralysis, Poison, or Sleep.

> The Pokemon releases medicinal scents and vapors to refresh the user and its allies.
