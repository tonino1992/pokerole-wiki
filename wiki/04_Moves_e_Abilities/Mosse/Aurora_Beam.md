# Aurora Beam
*Ice | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 1 Chance Dice to Reduce foe's Strength.

> The Pokemon shoots a beautiful rainbow colored beam that may leave the foe flabbergasted.
