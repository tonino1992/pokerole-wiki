# Bubble Beam
*Water | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 1 Chance Dice to Reduce the foe's Dexterity.

> A long stream of bubbles hits the foe, the bubble foam may prevent the target from moving freely.
