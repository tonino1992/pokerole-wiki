# Drum Beating
*Grass | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Perform
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Reduce the Foe's Dexterity

> The Pokemon has a perfectly good drum and you'd think it would use it. But it doesn't. It uses it's foe.
