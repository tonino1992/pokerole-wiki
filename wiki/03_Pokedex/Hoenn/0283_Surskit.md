# Surskit (#0283)

<img src="../../assets/images/pokemon/283.png" align="right" width="220" style="margin-left:20px;margin-bottom:8px;">

*Pond Skater Pokemon*

**Type:** Insetto / Acqua
**Abilities:** [[Swift Swim]], [[Rain Dish]] *(Hidden)*
**Base HP:** 3

> Surskit secretes oil from its feet to skate on water. It may be found on ponds, lakes and puddles in towns. The tip of its head produces a delicious sugary syrup often used in desserts and sweets.
