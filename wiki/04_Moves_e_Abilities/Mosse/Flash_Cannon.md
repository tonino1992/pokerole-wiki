# Flash Cannon
*Steel | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 1 Chance Dice to Reduce the foe's Sp. Defense.

> The Pokemon absorbs the reflecting light on its body, releasing a powerful beam against the foe.
