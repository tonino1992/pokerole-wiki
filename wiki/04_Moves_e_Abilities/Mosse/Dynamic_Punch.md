# Dynamic Punch
*Fighting | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 4
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Fist Based. Confuse the foe. -3 Accuracy.

> A punch that hits a small pressure point. It leavers the foe disoriented.
