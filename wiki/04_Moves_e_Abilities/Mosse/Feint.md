# Feint
*Normal | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 1
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Priority 2. This Move ignores any Shielding Move.

> The Pokemon feints a strike then hits for real even if the foe attempts to protect itself.
