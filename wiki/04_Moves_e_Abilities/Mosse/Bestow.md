# Bestow
*Normal | Support*

- **Accuracy:** Cool + Etiquette
- **Damage:** N/A
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** If target is not holding an Item, the User gives its own.

> The Pokemon respectfully offers an item, the target will take it and keep it to be polite.
