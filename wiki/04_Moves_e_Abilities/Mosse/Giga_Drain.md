# Giga Drain
*Grass | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** The user restores HP equal to half the damage dealt, rounded down.

> The Pokemon extracts a great amount of vital energy from the foe's body.
