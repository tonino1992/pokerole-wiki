# Draining Kiss
*Fairy | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** The User restores HP equal to half the damage dealt, rounded down.

> The user sends a kiss with a flirty intention, the foe catches the kiss but it ends up draining the target's energy.
