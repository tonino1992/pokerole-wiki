# Drain Punch
*Fighting | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Fist Based. The user restore HP equal to half the damage dealt, rounded down.

> The Pokemon strikes with a powerful punch. Through contact, it absorbs some vital energy.
