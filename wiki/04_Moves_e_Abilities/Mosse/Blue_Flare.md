# Blue Flare
*Fire | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Special + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 6
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 2 Chance Dice to Apply Burn 3 to the foe. -2 Accuracy.

> Reshiram surrounds itself with a giant blue flame that is unleashed with an earth shattering explosion. Receiving this attack directly might completely engulf you in fire.
