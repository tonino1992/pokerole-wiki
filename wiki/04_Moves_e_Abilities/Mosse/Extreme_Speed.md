# Extreme Speed
*Normal | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Priority 2. Triple the Pokemon's movement speed.

> The user charges at a blinding speed against the target.
