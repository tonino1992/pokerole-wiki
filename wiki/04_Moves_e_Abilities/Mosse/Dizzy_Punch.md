# Dizzy Punch
*Normal | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 2 Chance Dice to Confuse the foe. Fist Based.

> A swift punch to the jaw that spins the target and may leave it quite dizzy and disoriented.
