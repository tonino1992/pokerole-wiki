# Cover An Ally
*Typeless | Support*

- **Accuracy:** N/A
- **Damage:** N/A
- **Target:** One Ally
- **Effect:** The user will provide cover to the target from Ranged attacks and is more likely to be hit by close-range attacks. The level of Cover the user provides will depend on its size.
