# Darkest Lariat
*Dark | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Ignore the the foe's Attribute Increases that would decrease this Move's damage.

> The user swings both of its arms against the target, there's no armor thick enough to stop the strike.
