# Draco Meteor
*Dragon | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Special + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 6
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Lethal. Reduce User's Special by 2. -1 Accuracy.

> The Pokemon calls a comet that falls from the sky to deal a brutal amount of damage. This feat demands a lot from the user.
