# Bone Rush
*Ground | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 1
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Successive Actions. -1 Accuracy.

> The user quickly strikes the foe with a bone, one blow after another.
