# Attack Order
*Bug | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Tough + Nature
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** High Critical. Ranged.

> The user calls upon her swarm and heeds them to charge against you. No matter where you run, they will surround you. Hope you are not allergic to bees.
