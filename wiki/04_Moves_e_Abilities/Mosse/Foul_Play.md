# Foul Play
*Dark | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** S.T.A.B of the user and Strength of the foe apply for this move's damage.

> The user feint attacks then tricks the foe who ends up hurting itself.
