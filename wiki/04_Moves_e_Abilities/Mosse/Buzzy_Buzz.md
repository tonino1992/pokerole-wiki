# Buzzy Buzz
*Electric | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Paralyze the foe. If the user of this Move is at its Final Stage of Evolution, this move fails.

> The Pokemon releases a jolt of static electricity from its fur, that makes a cute zapping sound. It's all fun and games until somebody gets paralyzed.
