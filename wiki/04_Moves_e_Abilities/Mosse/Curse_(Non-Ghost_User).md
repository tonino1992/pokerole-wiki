# Curse (Non-Ghost User)
*Ghost | Support*

- **Accuracy:** Will + Channel
- **Damage:** N/A
- **Target:** User
- **Effect:** Increase the User's Strength and Defense. Reduce the User's Dexterity. This effect is only for Pokemon who are not Ghost-Type.

> The Pokemon prepares to deal and receive damage by muttering something not appropriate for kids.
