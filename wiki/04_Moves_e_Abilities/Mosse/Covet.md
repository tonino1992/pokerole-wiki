# Covet
*Normal | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Clever + Stealth
- **Damage:** Strength + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** If the user is not holding an item, Steal the target's Held Item.

> The User approaches with caution, deals a quick strike and takes the chance to steal the target's item.
