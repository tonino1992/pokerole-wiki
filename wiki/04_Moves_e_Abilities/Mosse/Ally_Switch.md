# Ally Switch
*Psychic | Support*

- **Accuracy:** Special + Channel
- **Damage:** N/A
- **Target:** One Ally
- **Effect:** User switches back. Choose another Pokemon to take its place. It will be ready to fight on the next Round. Switcher Move.

> The user teleports, switching its place with someone.
