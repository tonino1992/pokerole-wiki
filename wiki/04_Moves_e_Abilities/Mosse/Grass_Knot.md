# Grass Knot
*Grass | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 1
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Add 1 Extra Dice to the Damage Pool for every 50 kgs. of weight on the foe, up to +4 Extra Dice.

> The user snares the target's feet with grass, making it fall to the ground. The bigger the foe the harder the fall.
