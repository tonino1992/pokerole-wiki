# Grapple
*Typeless | Support*

- **Accuracy:** Strength + Brawl
- **Damage:** N/A
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** The foe is Blocked. Foe can resist this Maneuver by rolling their Strength or Dexterity score and scoring the same or more successes than the user.
