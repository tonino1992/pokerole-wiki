# Air Cutter
*Flying | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 2
- **Target:** All Foes
- **Effect:** Target all foes in Range. High Critical.

> The user will send out a wave of sharp wind that will harm anything it touches.
