# Chatter
*Flying | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Insight + Perform
- **Damage:** Special + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 3 Chance Dice to Confuse the foe. Sound based move.

> The user starts talking and giving orders in human language. It may be extremely confusing for the foes.
