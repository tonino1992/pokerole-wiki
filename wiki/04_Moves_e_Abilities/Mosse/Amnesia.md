# Amnesia
*Psychic | Support*

- **Accuracy:** Insight + Alert
- **Damage:** N/A
- **Target:** User
- **Effect:** Increase the User's Sp. Defense by 2.

> As a fine example of mind over matter, the user temporarily empties its mind to make it more resilient.
