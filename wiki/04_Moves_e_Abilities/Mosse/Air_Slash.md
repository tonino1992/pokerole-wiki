# Air Slash
*Flying | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 3 Chance Dice to Flinch the foe.

> The user will surround the foe with razor-like wind that may knock it down.
