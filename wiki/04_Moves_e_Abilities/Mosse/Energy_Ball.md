# Energy Ball
*Grass | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 1 Chance Dice to Reduce foe's Sp. Defense.

> The Pokemon gathers nature's energy in a ball and hurls it at the foe. Trees and bushes may be left opaque and sad.
