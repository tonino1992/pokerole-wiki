# Fake Tears
*Dark | Support*

- **Accuracy:** Cute + Perform
- **Damage:** N/A
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Reduce the foe's Sp. Defense by 2.

> The user stops battling and feigns to be crying, disrupting the foe's mood to battle and making it lower its guard.
