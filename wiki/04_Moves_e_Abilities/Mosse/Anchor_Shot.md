# Anchor Shot
*Steel | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Ranged. Blocks.

> The Pokemon hurls an anchor towards its foe and entangles it with seaweed, the anchor drags the foe down so it cannot escape.
