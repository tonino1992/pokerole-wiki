# Pidgeot (#0018)

<img src="../../assets/images/pokemon/018.png" align="right" width="220" style="margin-left:20px;margin-bottom:8px;">

*Bird Pokemon*

**Type:** Normal / Flying
**Abilities:** [[Keen Eye]], [[Tangled Feet]], [[Big Pecks]] *(Hidden)*
**Base HP:** 5
