# Constrict
*Normal | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 1
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Blocks. Roll 2 Dice of Damage against the foe at the end of each Round. Lasts 4 Rounds.

> The Pokemon uses its body to hold and constrict the foe. It won't let go easily.
