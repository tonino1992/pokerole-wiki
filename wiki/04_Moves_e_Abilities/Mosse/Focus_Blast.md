# Focus Blast
*Fighting | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Special + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 5
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 1 Chance Dice to Reduce foe's Sp. Defense. -2 Accuracy.

> The user concentrates and releases a blast of energy.
