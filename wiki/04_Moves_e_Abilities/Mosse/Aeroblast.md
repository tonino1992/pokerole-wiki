# Aeroblast
*Flying | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Special + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 4
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Lethal. High Critical.

> The victim is trapped in a violently swirling vortex. The strong wind currents make debris fly everywhere.
