# Chip Away
*Normal | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** This attack ignores Defense and Evasion modifiers on the foe.

> The user looks for an opening and strikes directly there. It nibbles bit by bit even the toughest of surfaces.
