# Extrasensory
*Psychic | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 1 Chance Dice to Flinch the foe.

> The user attacks with an odd, almost invisible power.
