# Apple Acid
*Grass | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Reduce the Foe's Sp. Defense.

> The Pokemon produces a sap from the tart apples it feeds on, if the apples, this sap is acidic and bad for your teeth.
