# Flying Press
*Fighting | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** When Dealing damage with this move, it counts as if this move was also Flying Type.

> The user flies towards the foe from the sky in pure Lucha Libre style.
