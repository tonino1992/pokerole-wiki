# Disable
*Normal | Support*

- **Accuracy:** Insight + Channel
- **Damage:** N/A
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** The Target cannot use the last move it performed. Lasts 4 Rounds.

> The user hits a nerve on the foe, impeding it from performing its previous action for a short while.
