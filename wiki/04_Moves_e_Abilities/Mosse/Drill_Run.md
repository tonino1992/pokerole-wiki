# Drill Run
*Ground | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** High Critical.

> The user rotates its body imitating a drill and crashes against the foe. This attack may pierce through walls and floors with ease and great precision.
