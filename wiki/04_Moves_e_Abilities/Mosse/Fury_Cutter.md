# Fury Cutter
*Bug | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 1
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Successive Actions.

> The Pokemon makes use of its claws or scythes to land as many hits as possible.
