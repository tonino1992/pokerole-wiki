# Dragon Rush
*Dragon | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 4
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 2 Chance Dice to Flinch the foe. -2 Accuracy.

> The user charges rapidly against the foe. The strike may leave thetarget gasping for air.
