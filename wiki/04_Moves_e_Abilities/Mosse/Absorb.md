# Absorb
*Grass | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 1
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** The user restores HP equal to half the damage dealt rounded down.

> The user absorbs the vital energy of the foe.
