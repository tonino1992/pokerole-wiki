# False Swipe
*Normal | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** The foe can't go below 1 HP when hit by this Move.

> The user strikes the foe but lowers the power of the blow at the last second.
