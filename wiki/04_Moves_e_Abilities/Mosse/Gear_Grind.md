# Gear Grind
*Steel | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Double Action. -1 Accuracy.

> The user traps the foe between its gears and crushes it with two painful grinds.
