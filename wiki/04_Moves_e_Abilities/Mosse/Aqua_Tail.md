# Aqua Tail
*Water | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 3 Chance Dice to Flinch the foe. -1 Accuracy.

> The user swings its tail as if it were a vicious wave in a raging storm.
