# Fusion Bolt
*Electric | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Strength + Channel
- **Damage:** Strength + 4
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Lethal. If the Move “Fusion Flare” has already been used by anyone this Round, add 4 Extra Dice to the Damage Pool of this Move.

> The Pokemon strikes with a devastating electric impact, said impact will grow larger and stronger if there's a special kind of fire nearby.
