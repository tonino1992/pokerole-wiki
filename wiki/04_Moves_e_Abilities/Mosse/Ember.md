# Ember
*Fire | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 1 Chance Dice to Burn the foe.

> The user shoots a small flame against the target that may cause a first-degree burn.
