# Freeze Dry
*Ice | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Water type Pokemon are dealt 2 Extra Damage.

> The user freezes all moisture in the environment, water type Pokemon suffer greatly.
