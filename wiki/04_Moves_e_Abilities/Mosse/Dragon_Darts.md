# Dragon Darts
*Dragon | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Strength + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Ranged. Double Action

> The Pokemon's head is a nest for its less evolved forms, the younglings will sometimes be used as projectiles.
