# Crunch
*Dark | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Lethal. Roll 1 Chance Dice to reduce foe's Defense.

> The Pokemon uses its fangs to viciously tear whatever it is biting.
