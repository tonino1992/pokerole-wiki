# Cross Chop
*Fighting | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 4
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** High Critical. -2 Accuracy.

> The user delivers a chop with its arms crossed trying to hot a weak spot.
