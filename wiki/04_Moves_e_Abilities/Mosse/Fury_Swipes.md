# Fury Swipes
*Normal | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 1
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Successive Actions. -1 Accuracy.

> The user swiftly uses its claws to strike the foe multiple times.
