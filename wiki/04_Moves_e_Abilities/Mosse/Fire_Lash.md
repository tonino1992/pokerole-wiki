# Fire Lash
*Fire | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Reduce Foe's Defense.

> With a whip of fire, the Pokemon ensnares its foe, who is left defenseless and at its mercy.
