# Fury Attack
*Normal | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 1
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Successive Actions. -1 Accuracy.

> The Pokemon goes at it with everything its got in a rather disordered way.
