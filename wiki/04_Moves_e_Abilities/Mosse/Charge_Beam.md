# Charge Beam
*Electric | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 5 Chance Dice to Increase the User's Special. -1 Accuracy.

> The Pokemon lets out an electric beam then uses the remaining charge to boost its power.
