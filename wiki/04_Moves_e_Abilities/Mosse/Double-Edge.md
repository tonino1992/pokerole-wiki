# Double-Edge
*Normal | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Strength + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 5
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Recoil.

> The user recklessly slams its foe to deal a lot of damage, but it may also hurt itself in the process.
