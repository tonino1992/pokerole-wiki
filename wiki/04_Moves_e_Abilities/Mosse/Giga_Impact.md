# Giga Impact
*Normal | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Strength + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 6
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Must Recharge. Lethal. -1 Accuracy.

> The Pokemon strikes with a brutal force using all its body. The impact leaves the user somewhat stunned.
