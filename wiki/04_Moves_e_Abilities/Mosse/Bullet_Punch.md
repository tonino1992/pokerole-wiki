# Bullet Punch
*Steel | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Fist Based. Priority 1.

> The user launches itself against the foe to strike it with an iron fist.
