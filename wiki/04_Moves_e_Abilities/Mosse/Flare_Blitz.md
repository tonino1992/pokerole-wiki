# Flare Blitz
*Fire | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Strength + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 5
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Recoil. Roll 1 Chance Dice to Apply Burn 3 to the foe.

> The user sets itself on fire, recklessly charging against the target with a brutal strike.
