# Final Gambit
*Fighting | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Will + Athletic
- **Damage:** N/A
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Ignore the foe's Defenses. The user faints after dealing damage.

> The Pokemon puts its last strength into an all-or-nothing attack. After that, it faints.
