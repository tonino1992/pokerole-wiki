# Crush Claw
*Normal | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 5 Chance Dice to Reduce the target's Defense by 2. Lethal.

> The user crushes its claws against the foe, it may pierce the skin of the foe.
