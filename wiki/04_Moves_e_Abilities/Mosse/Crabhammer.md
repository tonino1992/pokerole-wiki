# Crabhammer
*Water | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** High Critical. -1 Accuracy.

> A large pincer strikes with the power of a hammer. The demolition power is incredible.
