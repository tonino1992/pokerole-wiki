# Blast Burn
*Fire | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Special + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 6
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Lethal. Must Recharge. -1 Accuracy.

> The Pokemon uses all of its power to deliver an infernal blast to the foe. The user is left exhausted.
