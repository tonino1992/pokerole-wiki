# Aura Sphere
*Fighting | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 3
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Never Fail.

> The user channels its aura and launches this power in the form of a sphere The foe's aura is hit, leaving the target unscratched but deeply hurt.
