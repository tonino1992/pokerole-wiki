# Focus Punch
*Fighting | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Strength + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 6
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Fist Based. Charge Move. The User will flinch if it gets hit before releasing this attack.

> A brutal punch that requires calm and concentration
