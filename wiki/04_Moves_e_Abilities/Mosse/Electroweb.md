# Electroweb
*Electric | Special*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Channel
- **Damage:** Special + 2
- **Target:** All Foes
- **Effect:** Reduce Dexterity of those affected.

> Shoots an electrified web at all foes to restrict their movement. Walking through this web can be painful.
