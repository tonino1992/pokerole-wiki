# Force Palm
*Fighting | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 2
- **Target:** Foe
- **Effect:** Roll 3 Chance Dice to Paralyze the foe.

> A powerful open palm hit that knocks the foe away and may leave him unable to move.
