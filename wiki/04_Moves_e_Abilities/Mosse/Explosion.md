# Explosion
*Normal | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Strength + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 10
- **Target:** Area
- **Effect:** After the damage is dealt the User faints. Lethal.

> The user focuses on exploding its body, the explosion deals a massive wave of damage. A very dangerous move.
