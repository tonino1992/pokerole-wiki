# Brutal Swing
*Dark | Physical*

- **Accuracy:** Dexterity + Brawl
- **Damage:** Strength + 2
- **Target:** All Foes
- **Effect:** Lethal. Target all Foes in range.

> The Pokemon starts attacking at full force with its claws, anyone unfortunate enough to be in its path will end up severely wounded.
